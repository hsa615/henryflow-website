Who We Are

Empowering small businesses through professional-grade management tools.

Our Mission

HenryFlow was founded on a simple belief: that small businesses deserve the same powerful tools as giant corporations. We specialize in creating intuitive, scalable, and high-performance software that eliminates administrative friction, allowing entrepreneurs to focus on what they do best—growing their business.

The Vision

Our goal is to build a comprehensive ecosystem of finance and inventory products that work in harmony. From the first lead captured in FlowCRM to the final stock check in InvenHelper, we provide the digital infrastructure for modern entrepreneurship.
