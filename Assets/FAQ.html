Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HenryFlow products.

What is HenryFlow? +
HenryFlow is a professional software suite designed specifically for small business owners to manage their inventory, finances, and customer relationships more effectively.
How does InvenHelper work? +
InvenHelper uses AI-driven stock tracking and automated alerts to ensure you never run out of critical products. It also simplifies the reordering process through smart templates.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud storage to ensure your business data remains private and protected.
Do you offer a free trial? +
We offer several pricing tiers, including a basic free version for very small startups to get them started with the essential tools.
Can I export my data from your tools? +
Absolutely. All our tools, including InvenHelper, support CSV export so you always own your data.
